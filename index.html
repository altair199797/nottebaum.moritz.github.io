PhD Candidate · University of Udine · DMIF

I am a PhD student in the Machine Learning and Perception Lab at the University of Udine, advised by Christian Micheloni. My research focuses on hardware-efficient neural network design for efficient inference — specifically, building vision backbones that are fast on real hardware beyond just counting multiply-accumulate operations.
